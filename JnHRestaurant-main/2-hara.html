About Hara

Hello! I'm Hara Anggraini, just call me Hara. I am a co-founder of J&H restaurants!

Apart from being the co-founder of J&H, I am just an ordinary girl who used to have many dreams. But after now, my goal is only to be a useful human being, especially useful for the people around me.

Speaking of hobbies, I have a passion for photography, ranging from photographing landscapes, animals, plants, the sky and so on. I also like reading fantasy, horror, and romance novels, maybe those are the only two things that are my hobbies. The rest are doing normal activities and lying down hehe.

What inspired us to open a J&H restaurant? Hmm, Anjeli and I didn't think about starting a restaurant. We created this website only to fulfill the task of one of the courses. The task was ordered to create a website project with a free theme. At first we just wanted to create a website about the introduction of typical Banten food. But after we thought about it, why don't we just create a restaurant website? So that's where the idea came from.

After we worked on this project, we started to think. In shaa Allah someday we want to work together to build a restaurant called J&H Restaurant. Aamiin,,,

Have some question? Reach me on my social media.
